Payroll Process Flow

Monthly Payroll Steps

  1. Create Payroll Entry — select company, department, dates
  2. Get Employees — auto-fetches eligible employees
  3. Create Salary Slips — generates individual salary slips
  4. Review & verify salary slips
  5. Submit Salary Slips — locks calculations
  6. Make Accrual Journal Entry — posts to GL
  7. Make Bank Entry — creates payment entry for bank transfer
